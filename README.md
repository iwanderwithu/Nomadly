# 🌍 Nomadly — Your Relocation OS

> The all-in-one relocation planner for expats and digital nomads.

Nomadly helps you navigate every step of an international move — from visa paperwork and document tracking to city cost comparisons and AI-powered relocation advice.

-----

## ✨ Features

- **Relocation Planner** — 5-phase timeline with tasks organized by visa, finance, housing, health, and logistics
- **Document Tracker** — Track status of every required document (Ready / In Progress / Not Started)
- **Budget Planner** — Compare cost of living across cities in Spain, Portugal, Mexico, and more
- **AI Advisor** — Ask questions about visas, housing, costs, and citizenship timelines
- **Activity Feed** — Real-time log of every action across your relocation
- **Notifications** — Stay on top of urgent tasks and document deadlines
- **Global Search** — Find any task or document instantly
- **Freemium Model** — Free tier with upgrade prompts for Pro features
